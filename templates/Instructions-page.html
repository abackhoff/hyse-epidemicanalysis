1. Click the download excel file button on the homepage. This will download an Excel template to your computer.
  2. Fill in the downloaded Excel template with your data.
  3. Click the upload Excel file button on the homepage and select the Excel file you just filled in.
  4. Choose what type of Statistical Process Control Analysis you would like to perform on your data from the available drawdown menu in the homepage.
  5. Click the View Charts button to view your desired results!

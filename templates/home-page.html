Overview

Statistical methods are used to detect new outbreaks or rapid changes in epidemic trajectories. They are often under-used and hard to use without technical expertise. The early detection of epidemic behaviour is essential for disease surveillance and control purposes. There are no existing user-friendly platfroms where users can input epidemic data and have this data be analyzed and displayed through data visualization models and Statistical methods. That is why we decided to approach this problem by developing a user-friendly platform that can model epidemic behavior and identify statistical changes in this behavior. This platform will allow you to input data and output statistical information including models and Statistical Process Control Charts (SPC) through an effective and flexible user interface.
